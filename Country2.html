Japan

Japanese culture is a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. It is defined by deep social respect, a pursuit of harmony, and a rich spiritual heritage that shapes daily life.⛩️ Religion and SpiritualityJapanese spirituality is highly syncretic, with two main practices coexisting peacefully:Shintoism: The indigenous religion focused on worshipping nature spirits called kami.Buddhism: Imported from China, deeply tied to meditation practices and funeral rituals.
